Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many events can a participant join?
    A participant can join a maximum of two events, including any two technical and two non-technical events. (It’s the team's responsibility to ensure that no events clash with one another)
  • What is the registration fee, and when is the payment deadline?
    The registration fee is ₹150 per participant, and it must be paid on or before 28th Nov 2024.
  • Who is eligible to participate in the event?
    Both undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) students are eligible to participate.
  • What documents must participants bring on the event day?
    Participants must bring their college ID card along with a bonafide certificate from their respective college.
  • How should participants register for the event? Are spot registrations allowed?
    Participants must register online, as spot registration is not encouraged.
  • Will food and refreshments be provided to participants?
    Yes, food and refreshments will be provided to participants.
